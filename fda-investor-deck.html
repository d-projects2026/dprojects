Risk Analysis
Key risks identified with mitigation strategies
Market Risk

Price corrections in residential market. Mitigated by conservative pricing 10-15% below luxury tier and strong pre-sales buffer.

Construction Cost Overrun

Material price inflation (steel, cement). Mitigated by 10% contingency and fixed-price contracts where possible.

Regulatory Delays

Permit processing timelines. Mitigated by early engagement with authorities and experienced local counsel.

Currency Depreciation

USD devaluation impacting imported materials. Mitigated by local sourcing strategy (85%+ domestic materials).

Sales Absorption

Slower than projected unit sales. Mitigated by flexible payment plans and phased release strategy.

Interest Rate Risk

Rising financing costs. Mitigated by securing fixed-rate facility and maintaining 40% equity cushion.
