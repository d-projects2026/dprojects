Frequently Asked Questions

What format are the templates?

Most templates are .XLSX, .DOCX, or .PPTX files that you can download and customize immediately. Some advanced dashboards are interactive Excel workbooks with data-piping and automation.

Can I use templates for my team?

Individual templates include personal use license. For team access, we offer SMB and Enterprise plans with unlimited users. Contact us for licensing details.

What is the difference between Suites and individual Templates?

Suites are complete, integrated packages (10-20 templates each) covering a full discipline. Individual Templates are standalone tools you can mix and match. Suites offer better value if you need a complete category.

Do you offer customization?

Yes! We can customize templates, build new suites, or integrate our tools into your systems. Contact our sales team for enterprise services.

What support is included?

Basic downloads include email support. SMB plans include priority support. Enterprise customers get 24/7 dedicated support with a project manager.
