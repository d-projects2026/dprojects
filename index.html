Who We Are

D. Projects is a professional construction management consultancy under the D. Insights family. We specialize in designing, configuring, and deploying complete project management systems for organizations across the MENA region and beyond.

Our 50 specialized suites are organized across 10 departments — from Project Controls and Procurement to Engineering, Development, Risk & Safety, People Management, Digital & Technology, Infrastructure, Interiors, and Sector-Specific Solutions. Each suite is purpose-built to address a specific aspect of project delivery, covering everything from data architecture and reporting systems to procurement workflows, compliance frameworks, and stakeholder management.

Our catalog showcases what we can deliver, but the real value lies in how we tailor each solution to your specific project type, team structure, delivery model, and business requirements. Whether you're a small consulting firm, a growing contractor, a major developer, or a government agency, we work with you to Identify your needs, Configure your package, Deploy integrated systems with training, and Scale as your project evolves. Every deliverable is branded, customized, and ready to drive professional project execution.
