THE PROBLEM

Why most construction PMOs struggle with reporting and data management.

01

Fragmented Reporting

Monthly reports built from scratch every cycle. No templates. No consistency. Hours wasted reformatting.

02

No Data Flow

Cost data lives in one spreadsheet, schedule in another, risk in a third. Nothing connects. Manual copy-paste everywhere.

03

Tool Overload

Expensive PMO software that nobody uses. Teams revert to Excel and Word because enterprise tools are too complex.

04

No Single Source of Truth

Multiple versions of the truth. The client sees one number, the PM sees another. Decisions made on stale data.
