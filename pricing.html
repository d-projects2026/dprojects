WHITE-LABEL BRANDING OPTIONS

All deliverables include D. Projects branding. Included in Tiers 1–3 by default. Standard watermarks and logos on templates, dashboards, and reports.

Remove D. Projects logo and apply your company's branding across all templates, dashboards, and reports. Available as add-on for Tier 4 and above.

Included at no extra cost in Premium and Strategic tiers. Complete rebranding with your identity, colors, logo, and custom domain integration.
