SUITE 6 — RECOVERY & CLAIMS

Project Recovery & Claims

Manage project delays, resolve disputes, document claims systematically, and recover troubled projects with integrated recovery planning and claims tracking — everything needed to navigate complex recovery scenarios and maximize claim recovery.

SUITE PRODUCTS

CLAIMS REGISTER

Claims Register

Comprehensive 3-sheet claims management workbook for tracking all project claims. The Claims Register sheet captures 8 detailed entries with claim types (EOT, VO, Delay, Cost Overrun, Force Majeure, Disruption, Acceleration), status, dates, and values, with 7 blank rows for additional claims. Claims Summary sheet uses COUNTIF formulas to auto-tally claims by type. Delay Analysis sheet documents as-planned vs as-built schedules with 46-day total delay analysis for delay claims substantiation.

  • 15 claim rows (8 samples + 7 blank)
  • 7 claim types: EOT, VO, Delay, Cost Overrun, Force Majeure, Disruption, Acceleration
  • Status tracking: Submitted / Approved / Under Review / Rejected
  • Claims Summary with auto-count by type
  • Delay Analysis with as-planned vs as-built comparison
  • 46-day total delay quantification

RECOVERY PLAN

Recovery Plan Template

2-sheet recovery planning workbook designed for project remediation. Recovery Actions sheet details 10 pre-populated recovery initiatives with priority levels (Critical/High/Medium), categories (Schedule/Cost/Quality/Resource/Commercial), implementation status, and cost impact. Recovery Summary sheet auto-calculates action counts by priority and category using COUNTIF and SUM formulas, providing executive visibility into recovery strategy effectiveness and cost implications.

  • 10 recovery action items with 5 blank rows
  • Priority levels: Critical / High / Medium
  • 5 categories: Schedule, Cost, Quality, Resource, Commercial
  • Status tracking: Planned / In Progress / Completed
  • Cost impact quantification per action
  • Summary with auto-calculated priority counts

Claims Timeline Dashboard

Interactive HTML dashboard for real-time claims management and visual timeline analysis. Displays 4 critical KPIs (8 claims, USD 24.9M value, 2 approved, 46-day delay impact). Features claims-by-type bar chart, claim status doughnut visualization, interactive timeline with status badges showing claim progression, delay analysis comparative bars, and monthly cumulative claim value line chart for trend forecasting and portfolio visibility.

  • 4 critical KPIs: Total claims, Value, Approved, Delay Days
  • Claims by type bar chart (EOT/VO/Delay/Cost/etc)
  • Claim status doughnut (Approved/Review/Submitted)
  • Visual timeline with status badges
  • Delay analysis comparative bars
  • Monthly cumulative value line chart
