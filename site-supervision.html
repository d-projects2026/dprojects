NCR REGISTER

NCR Register

Professional Non-Conformance Report (NCR) tracking workbook with 2 sheets. The NCR Log captures issues with severity levels (Critical, Major, Minor), status tracking (Open/In Progress/Closed), and includes 6 sample entries with 14 blank rows for new NCRs. NCR Summary sheet auto-tallies issues by severity and calculates close-out rate, enabling management dashboards and trend analysis.

  • 20 NCR rows (6 samples + 14 blank)
  • Severity dropdown: Critical / Major / Minor
  • Status tracking: Open / In Progress / Closed
  • NCR Summary with count by severity
  • Close-out rate and trend visibility
  • Owner and due date assignment
